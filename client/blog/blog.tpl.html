...And we're live!

Written by The Mojsart Team on June 1, 2014

We're thrilled to announce the beta launch of our genre visulization engine. Our three-person team created this over a week at Hack Reactor. Please try out the visualizer, add to the database, and provide song sentiment ratings in order to improve our model.

Since we're just getting started, we'd love it if you could provide feedback about the product. You can reach us through the email below, and you can check out the source for our project on GitHub.

Tech Stack
  • Front-end: AngularJS
  • Back-end: Express
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Song data: Echonest API
  • Visualization: D3.js

The Mojsart project

Written by The Mojsart Team on May 28, 2014.

Popular music webapps such as Pandora allow you to find music you like using complex algorithms to analyze many different properties of songs you like. It presents you with new songs, and gives you a basic reason as to why it thinks you may like this other song. Mojsart aims to do a similar thing, but to present such suggestions visually.

Feel free to follow our progress on GitHub!
